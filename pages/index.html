Announcing the 2020 Ideas for Action Competition

The World Bank Group and the Zicklin Center at Wharton are pleased to host the 2020 Ideas for Action (I4A) competition, which engages young people (18-35 years old) to develop innovative solutions in support of the implementation of the SDGs ( https://ideas4action.org ). Note that the Competition Guidelines for 2020 will mirror those for 2019 ( https://ideas4action.org/2019-competition/).
Additionally, please note that the deadline for submission has been extended to March 22nd, 2020. In 2019, we received 3,100 applications from 142 countries and territories, and more than 7,000 registered teams, Due to the increased interest, we are moving to a new platform for registration and applications submission, which will be available in the second half of November.
